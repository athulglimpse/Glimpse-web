Content Marketing in 2023: Top Trends and Best Practices for Creating Engaging Content

by Hashim Riyan | February 14, 2023

In contemporary digital marketing, the implementation of content marketing has emerged as an indispensable facet of businesses' promotional strategy. As we enter the new year of 2023, the role of creating captivating content has become even more paramount. In this essay, we will delve into the top trends and best practices that underpin the creation of engrossing content in 2023.

The foremost trend in content marketing is the integration of video content. With the advent of platforms such as TikTok and Instagram Reels, the popularity of video content has skyrocketed and is poised to increase even further. In fact, according to a report by Cisco, video content is projected to constitute 82% of all internet traffic by 2023. To engender scintillating video content, brands must emphasize the creation of content that is visually compelling and informative. The video should evoke emotions, elucidate a narrative, and offer value to the viewer.

A pivotal trend in content marketing is the personalization of content. Given the saturation of content accessible online, customizing content to align with the viewer's preferences has become more salient than ever. Personalized content can be achieved through data collection and analysis. Brands can employ data to comprehend their audience's inclinations and create content that is tailored to their specific needs. Interactive content, such as quizzes or surveys, also engenders personalized content.

In 2023, optimizing content for mobile devices has become essential. Mobile devices accounted for over 50% of website traffic in 2021, according to a report by Statista. Thus, brands must ensure that their content is optimized for mobile devices, including smartphones and tablets. This requires creating content that is easily readable on smaller screens, with succinct and comprehensible messaging. Brands should also consider creating content specifically for mobile devices, such as mobile apps or mobile-first websites.

Another pivotal trend in content marketing is the implementation of influencer marketing. Influencer marketing involves collaborating with influencers or content creators to promote a brand's products or services. Influencer marketing can be incredibly potent, as it enables brands to reach a highly engaged and targeted audience. In 2023, it is crucial for brands to collaborate with influencers whose values align with their brand and target audience. Brands should also consider partnering with micro-influencers, who have smaller but more devoted followings.

In addition to these trends, several best practices can be employed in creating engrossing content in 2023. Brands must concentrate on creating content that is high-quality and valuable to their audience. This entails focusing on creating content that is informative, entertaining, or offers solutions to problems. Long-form content, such as blog posts or e-books, often engenders more engaging and informative content than shorter-form content.

Employing storytelling is another fundamental best practice in creating captivating content. Storytelling is a potent tool for creating emotional connections with an audience. Brands must focus on creating stories that are relatable and evoke emotions such as joy, sadness, or empathy. Storytelling can be achieved through the use of video, written content, or even social media posts.

In 2023, creating content that is shareable has also become crucial for brands. Shareable content is content that people are more likely to share with their friends or on social media. To create shareable content, brands must focus on creating content that is visually alluring, informative, or entertaining. Social share buttons should also be added to content, making it facile for viewers to share the content on their social media platforms.

In conclusion, creating engrossing content has become an even more significant aspect of content marketing in 2023. Brands must focus on creating personalized, mobile-friendly, and shareable content that is of interest to their growing viewers and pertinent to their audience.
