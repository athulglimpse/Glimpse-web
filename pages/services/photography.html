Each shoot is approached with a clear creative brief, professional equipment, and expert direction to ensure the final images align with your brand's visual standards.

Yes. Commercial and brand photography is one of our core specialisations. We work with businesses across Dubai and the UAE to create high-quality visual content for websites, social media, marketing campaigns, advertising, and corporate communications. Our photography experts understand how to translate a brand's identity into compelling imagery ensuring every shot communicates the right message to the right audience.

Our professional photography team covers a wide range of events including corporate conferences and seminars, product launches, brand activations, networking events, exhibitions and trade shows, gala dinners, and cultural and government events. We are experienced in fast-paced event environments and deliver sharp, well-composed images that capture the energy and key moments of your occasion even in challenging lighting conditions.

Yes. We offer professional portrait and headshot photography sessions for individuals, executives, and teams. Whether you need polished corporate headshots for LinkedIn and company websites, personal branding portraits for entrepreneurs, or team photography for a growing business, our photographers bring expert lighting, direction, and post-production to every session. Sessions can be conducted in-studio or at your office or a preferred location in Dubai.

Session duration varies depending on the type and scope of the shoot. A corporate headshot session for a small team typically takes 1–2 hours. A half-day commercial shoot for a brand campaign runs 4–5 hours. Full event coverage can span an entire day or multiple days. We agree on the estimated shoot duration during the briefing stage and plan the session schedule to make the most of every hour with no rushed shots or missed moments.

Yes. Professional post-production is included in every photography engagement. Our editing process covers colour grading and correction, exposure balancing, retouching, background cleanup, and format optimisation for your intended use whether that is web, print, social media, or advertising. We do not simply deliver raw files. Every image is reviewed and edited to a consistent professional standard before delivery.

Delivery timelines depend on the volume and complexity of the shoot. For standard sessions, edited images are typically delivered within 5–7 business days. For larger commercial projects or event coverage with a high volume of images, we agree on a specific delivery timeline at the briefing stage. Rush delivery is available upon request. All images are delivered via a secure online gallery or your preferred file transfer method.

Yes. Creative direction is an integral part of how we approach photography at Glimpse. Before every shoot, our team develops a detailed mood board and shot list aligned to your brand and campaign objectives. We can coordinate art direction, wardrobe styling, prop sourcing, location scouting, and talent or model coordination as required. This full pre-production process is what separates professional photography services from basic photography and it is what ensures the final images are exactly what your brief intended.
