A branding agency helps businesses define, build, and communicate who they are beyond just a logo. At Glimpse, our branding work covers brand strategy (positioning, purpose, values), visual identity (logo, color palette, typography, imagery style), brand voice and messaging, and brand experience design across digital and physical touchpoints. The goal is to create a brand that resonates deeply with your target audience and differentiates you clearly from competitors.

As a full-service branding agency in Dubai, Glimpse offers brand strategy and positioning, logo and visual identity design, brand guidelines and style systems, packaging design, brand naming, rebranding, brand experience design, and campaign branding for product launches and activations. Whether you are building a brand from scratch or evolving an existing one, we have the expertise to deliver.

These terms are often used interchangeably. A branding agency typically offers end-to-end brand strategy and creative execution. A branding firm may lean more toward consultancy and strategic advisory. A branding company is a broader term covering any business that provides brand-related services. At Glimpse, we operate as a full-service branding partner combining strategic rigour with award-winning creative output to deliver measurable brand impact.

Our brand identity process begins with a deep discovery phase, understanding your business goals, target audience, competitive landscape, and brand personality. From there, we develop a brand strategy that defines your positioning and messaging framework. Our creative team then translates that strategy into a visual identity system: logo, typography, color palette, imagery style, and design language. Everything is documented in a comprehensive brand guidelines deck for consistent application.

Timelines vary depending on the scope of work. A focused visual identity project for a new brand typically takes 6–10 weeks. A full brand strategy and identity system for a larger organisation can range from 12 to 20 weeks. Rebranding projects with internal alignment and phased rollouts may take longer. We agree on a clear timeline and milestones at the start of every engagement so there are no surprises.

Brand strategy is the foundational thinking that drives all brand decisions it defines who you are, what you stand for, who you serve, and how you are different from competitors. Without a clear strategy, even the most beautiful visual identity will struggle to connect with audiences or drive business results. At Glimpse, strategy always comes before design. It is what ensures your brand is not just attractive, but meaningful and effective.
