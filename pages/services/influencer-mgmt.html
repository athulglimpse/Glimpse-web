Frequently Asked Questions

Influencer marketing is a form of social media marketing that partners brands with creators who have established, engaged audiences on platforms like Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Snapchat. Rather than advertising directly to consumers, brands leverage the trust and credibility influencers have built with their followers to promote products or services authentically. In Dubai and the UAE, influencer marketing is especially powerful the region has one of the highest social media penetration rates in the world, and consumers here are highly receptive to creator-led recommendations across lifestyle, beauty, fashion, food, real estate, and technology categories.

As a full-service influencer marketing agency in Dubai, Glimpse offers end-to-end influencer campaign services including influencer strategy and campaign planning, creator identification and audience vetting, outreach and contract negotiation, creative brief development and content direction, content review and brand approval, campaign execution and scheduling, performance tracking and post-campaign reporting, and long-term influencer ambassador programme management. Whether you need a one-off product launch activation or an always-on influencer marketing programme, we manage every element from strategy through to results.

Our influencer management services cover the complete lifecycle of brand-creator relationships. We identify and vet influencers based on audience demographics, engagement authenticity, content quality, and brand alignment. We handle all outreach, rate negotiation, and contractual agreements on your behalf. Once onboarded, we brief each creator with clear campaign objectives and brand guidelines, review content before publication for compliance and quality, track all deliverables, and manage ongoing communication throughout the campaign. Influencer management removes the significant time and operational burden of working with multiple creators simultaneously ensuring every partnership is seamless, professional, and on-brand.

We work across all influencer tiers, each suited to different campaign objectives. Nano influencers (1K–10K followers) offer the highest engagement rates and strongest audience trust ideal for hyper-targeted, authentic advocacy. Micro influencers (10K–100K) balance reach with strong engagement and category credibility. Macro influencers (100K–1M) deliver broad awareness with established platform authority. Mega influencers and celebrities (1M+) provide mass cultural reach for major brand moments and campaign launches. We recommend the right tier mix based on your specific goals, audience, and budget never simply the biggest numbers available.

We plan and execute influencer campaigns across all major social media platforms active in the UAE and MENA region. Instagram is our most active channel, covering Reels, Stories, and Feed posts for lifestyle, fashion, beauty, and e-commerce brands. TikTok is the platform of choice for reaching Gen Z and younger Millennial audiences with short-form video content. Influencer vetting is one of the most important stages of our process and one of the areas where many brands make costly mistakes when working with creators directly. We assess every influencer across six criteria: audience demographics (age, gender, location, interests), follower authenticity (detecting fake or inactive followers using audience analysis tools), engagement rate relative to follower count, content quality and visual consistency, previous brand partnership history and category relevance, and personal brand values and reputation. An influencer with 500K followers but a 0.5% engagement rate and an inauthentic audience will deliver far less value than a micro influencer with 50K highly engaged, relevant followers. We ensure every creator we recommend meets our standards across all six criteria before presenting them to your brand.

Authenticity is the single most important factor in influencer marketing effectiveness and it is what separates high-performing campaigns from those that audiences scroll past. We develop creative briefs that communicate your brand objectives, key messages, and mandatory inclusions clearly while giving influencers significant creative latitude to communicate in their own voice. We never provide word-for-word scripts. We select creators who have a genuine interest in or connection to your brand category. For long-term ambassador programmes, we build ongoing relationships that allow influencers to speak about your brand with real familiarity over time the type of advocacy that audiences trust most.

We define campaign KPIs before any influencer is briefed, aligned to your specific business objective. For awareness campaigns, we track reach, impressions, views, and share of voice. For engagement campaigns, we measure likes, comments, saves, shares, and engagement rate per creator. For conversion campaigns, we track link clicks, website sessions, leads, and sales using UTM-tagged links, custom landing pages, and unique discount or affiliate codes per influencer. Post-campaign, clients receive a detailed performance report covering overall campaign results, individual creator performance, top-performing content, audience response analysis, and strategic recommendations for the next campaign cycle.
