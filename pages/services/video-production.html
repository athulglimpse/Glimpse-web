Our professional video production process covers three core stages. Pre-production includes creative briefing, concept development, scriptwriting, storyboarding, location scouting, talent coordination, and shoot scheduling. Production involves the full filming day camera crew, lighting, sound, direction, and on-set art direction. Post-production covers video editing, colour grading, sound design, music licensing, motion graphics, subtitling, and format optimisation for each intended platform. Every stage is managed by our team with regular client touchpoints and approval milestones.

Production timelines vary based on the complexity and scope of the project. A short-form social media video or product clip typically takes 1–2 weeks from brief to delivery. Larger documentary or multi-location productions can take 6–10 weeks or more. We agree on a clear production schedule with milestones and delivery dates at the briefing stage so every deadline is planned in advance.
