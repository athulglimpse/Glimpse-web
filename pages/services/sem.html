Frequently Asked Questions

Search Engine Marketing (SEM) refers to paid advertising on search engines, primarily Google Ads and Bing Ads, where you bid to appear at the top of search results for specific keywords. SEO, on the other hand, focuses on earning organic (unpaid) rankings over time. SEM delivers immediate visibility and measurable ROI, while SEO builds long-term authority. At Glimpse, we often recommend running both together for maximum search coverage.

We manage the full spectrum of paid search services: Google Search Ads, Google Display Network, Google Shopping Ads, YouTube Ads, Performance Max campaigns, and remarketing. From initial strategy and campaign architecture to ad copywriting, bid management, and ongoing optimisation, we handle every layer of your paid search presence.

Glimpse is a MENA Agency of the Year (2022–2023) with over a decade of experience running high-performance paid search campaigns across the UAE and wider region. We don't just set up campaigns and walk away — we continuously test, optimise, and scale based on real business results. Our team is certified in Google Ads and brings cross-industry expertise to every account.

We follow a proven campaign architecture process: market and competitor research, keyword mapping, audience segmentation, ad group structuring, ad copy creation, landing page alignment, conversion tracking setup, and bid strategy selection. Every campaign is built for quality score optimisation from the start, which directly impacts your cost-per-click and ad placement.

Scaling a paid search campaign means increasing its reach and return without proportionally increasing cost. We achieve this through performance data analysis, expanding to high-intent keyword variants, improving Quality Scores to lower CPCs, refining audience targeting, and introducing new ad formats like Performance Max or Dynamic Search Ads. Scaling is a deliberate, data-led process not simply raising the budget.

We define success metrics before any campaign launches, aligned to your goals whether that is leads, sales, app installs, or ROAS (Return on Ad Spend). Key metrics we track include Click-Through Rate (CTR), Cost Per Click (CPC), Conversion Rate, Cost Per Acquisition (CPA), and overall campaign ROI. Clients receive regular performance dashboards and review sessions to stay fully informed.
