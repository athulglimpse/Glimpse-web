Frequently Asked Questions

Glimpse offers a full suite of social media services in Dubai including Social Media Strategy and Planning, Social Media Management (content calendar, publishing, community management), Social Media Marketing (organic growth and audience building), Social Media Advertising (paid campaigns across Meta, Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat, LinkedIn, and YouTube), content creation (copywriting, design, video reels), influencer coordination, and monthly performance reporting. Whether you need a single service or a fully managed social media presence, we build the engagement around your business goals.

Social media management refers to the day-to-day operation of your social accounts creating and scheduling content, responding to comments and messages, maintaining your brand voice, and growing your community organically. Social media marketing is the broader strategic discipline that includes both organic management and paid advertising to amplify reach, drive traffic, and generate leads or sales. At Glimpse, we offer both as standalone services or as an integrated social media solution that covers strategy, management, advertising, and reporting under one roof.

We manage and run paid campaigns across all major social media platforms active in the UAE and MENA region Instagram, Facebook (Meta), TikTok, Snapchat, LinkedIn, YouTube, and X (Twitter). Platform selection is driven by your audience demographics and business objectives. For example, Snapchat holds strong market penetration in Saudi Arabia and the Gulf, TikTok dominates younger demographics, and LinkedIn is the platform of choice for B2B and professional services targeting.

Glimpse is a MENA Agency of the Year (2022–2023) with over a decade of experience managing social media for brands across the UAE and wider region. We do not outsource content or management everything is handled by our in-house team of strategists, copywriters, designers, and paid media specialists. This means your brand voice stays consistent, your content stays on-strategy, and your campaigns are optimised continuously. Our clients do not just grow followers they build engaged communities that convert.

We handle end-to-end content creation as part of our social media management service. This includes copywriting in English and Arabic (where required), graphic design, short-form video and Reels production, and story and carousel formats. We develop a monthly content calendar for client review and approval before anything goes live. If you have existing brand assets or photography you would like us to use, we incorporate those seamlessly into the content plan.

Our social media advertising service covers the full paid campaign lifecycle: audience research and targeting strategy, ad creative production (static, video, carousel, story formats), campaign setup and pixel/tracking configuration, A/B testing, ongoing bid and budget optimisation, and detailed performance reporting. We run campaigns across Meta Ads Manager, TikTok Ads, Snapchat Ads, LinkedIn Campaign Manager, and YouTube always aligned to clear KPIs such as reach, engagement, website traffic, leads, or ROAS.

We define success metrics before any campaign or management period begins, aligned to your specific business goals. For organic social media management, we track follower growth, reach, impressions, engagement rate, and content performance by format. For social media advertising, we measure click-through rate (CTR), cost per click (CPC), cost per lead (CPL), conversion rate, and return on ad spend (ROAS). Clients receive monthly performance reports with clear commentary on results, insights, and the optimisations we are implementing for the next period.
